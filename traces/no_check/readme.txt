traces/no_check

This directory contains trace files generated by tools/launcher/launcher_driver.sh,
which executes tools/launcher/launcher with different command-line arguments to
explore the behavior of programs under the regular cgroup-v1 real-time group 
scheduling and "no-check" group scheduling.

There are two subdirectories are no_check_rt_group_uclamp and rt_group_uclamp.
They refer to following kernels:
